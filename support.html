Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link shortly.

How do I manage my subscription?
You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time via your device's App Store settings. On iPhone, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?
You can request account and data deletion by visiting our Account Deletion page.

I'm having trouble logging in — what should I do?
Try resetting your password first. If you're still having trouble, email us at support@pt360app.com and we'll help you regain access.
